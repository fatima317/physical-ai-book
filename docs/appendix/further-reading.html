Further Reading

This section provides additional resources for deepening your understanding of Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics topics.

Books​

Core Robotics​

  • "Robotics, Vision and Control" by Peter Corke

    • Comprehensive coverage of robotics fundamentals with practical MATLAB examples
    • Covers kinematics, dynamics, control, and computer vision

  • "Introduction to Robotics: Mechanics and Control" by John J. Craig

    • Classic textbook on robot mechanics and control
    • Excellent for understanding kinematics and dynamics

  • "Probabilistic Robotics" by Sebastian Thrun, Wolfram Burgard, and Dieter Fox

    • In-depth treatment of probabilistic methods in robotics
    • Essential for understanding localization, mapping, and planning

AI and Machine Learning for Robotics​

  • "Robot Learning" by Andrew Barto and Richard Sutton

    • Foundational text on reinforcement learning for robotics
    • Covers learning algorithms for robot control

  • "Computer Vision: Algorithms and Applications" by Richard Szeliski

    • Comprehensive guide to computer vision techniques
    • Important for robot perception systems

  • "Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning" by Christopher Bishop

    • Comprehensive introduction to machine learning
    • Essential for understanding AI techniques in robotics

ROS-Specific Books​

  • "Programming Robots with ROS" by Morgan Quigley, Brian Gerkey, and William Smart

    • Practical guide to ROS programming
    • Excellent examples and best practices

  • "Effective Robotics Programming with ROS" by Anil Mahtani, Luis Sanchez Crespo, and Enrique Fernandez

    • Advanced ROS programming techniques
    • Covers real-world robotics applications

Research Papers​

Foundational Papers​

  • "The Robot Operating System" (Quigley et al., 2009)

    • Original ROS paper describing the architecture and design
    • Essential reading for understanding ROS concepts

  • "ROS: an open-source robot operating system" (Roberson et al., 2009)

    • Early paper describing the ROS framework
    • Historical context for ROS development

State Estimation and SLAM​

  • "A Tutorial on Graph-Based SLAM" (Grisetti et al., 2007)

    • Comprehensive introduction to SLAM algorithms
    • Essential for understanding robot localization

  • "Real-Time Loop Closure in 2D LIDAR SLAM" (Hess et al., 2016)

    • Description of Cartographer, a popular SLAM system
    • Important for practical SLAM implementation

Deep Learning for Robotics​

  • "End-to-End Learning of Driving Models from Large-Scale Video Datasets" (Bojarski et al., 2016)

    • Early work on end-to-end learning for control
    • Influential for modern robot learning approaches

  • "Deep Reinforcement Learning for Robotic Manipulation" (Gu et al., 2017)

    • Application of deep RL to robotic manipulation
    • Important for learning-based robot control

Human-Robot Interaction​

  • "A Survey of Socially Interactive Robots" (Belpaeme et al., 2018)

    • Comprehensive review of social robotics
    • Important for understanding HRI principles

  • "Robot Learning from Demonstration: A Survey" (Schaal, 1999)

    • Foundational paper on learning from demonstration
    • Key approach for robot programming

Journals​

Top Robotics Journals​

  • IEEE Transactions on Robotics (T-RO)

    • Premier journal for robotics research
    • Covers all aspects of robotics

  • The International Journal of Robotics Research (IJRR)

    • High-impact journal covering fundamental robotics research
    • Historical and cutting-edge robotics work

  • Autonomous Robots

    • Focuses on autonomous systems and robot intelligence
    • Emphasizes practical implementation

  • Journal of Field Robotics

    • Focuses on robots operating in real-world environments
    • Important for practical robotics applications

AI and Machine Learning Journals​

  • Journal of Machine Learning Research (JMLR)

    • Top journal for machine learning research
    • Important for AI techniques in robotics

  • IEEE Transactions on Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence (TPAMI)

    • Leading journal for computer vision and pattern recognition
    • Critical for robot perception

Conference Proceedings​

Major Robotics Conferences​

  • IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA)

    • Premier robotics conference
    • Covers all aspects of robotics research

  • International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS)

    • Major robotics conference focusing on intelligent systems
    • Practical and theoretical robotics work

  • Robotics: Science and Systems (RSS)

    • High-quality single-track robotics conference
    • Emphasizes theoretical foundations

  • International Symposium on Robotics Research (ISRR)

    • Selective symposium on fundamental robotics research
    • High-quality, in-depth robotics work

AI and Machine Learning Conferences​

  • Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS)

    • Premier conference for machine learning
    • Increasingly relevant for robot learning

  • International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML)

    • Top machine learning conference
    • Important for learning algorithms in robotics

  • IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR)

    • Leading computer vision conference
    • Critical for robot perception

Online Resources​

Courses and Tutorials​

  • MIT 6.832 Underactuated Robotics

    • Advanced robotics course covering optimal control
    • Excellent for understanding robot control theory

  • Carnegie Mellon 16-745 Optimal Control and Planning

    • Graduate-level course on robot planning and control
    • Important for advanced robot behavior

  • ETH Zurich Advanced Dynamics and Control of Robotic Systems

    • Advanced course on robot dynamics and control
    • Essential for understanding robot mechanics

Technical Blogs and Articles​

  • The Robot Report

    • Industry news and technical articles
    • Good for understanding commercial robotics

  • IEEE Spectrum Robotics

    • News and analysis of robotics research and applications
    • Covers both academic and industrial robotics

  • ROS Discourse Announcements

    • Official announcements and technical discussions
    • Important for staying updated on ROS developments

Software Libraries and Frameworks​

Computer Vision​

  • OpenCV Documentation and Tutorials

    • Essential for computer vision in robotics
    • Extensive examples and algorithms

  • Open3D Documentation

    • 3D data processing library
    • Important for point cloud processing

  • PCL (Point Cloud Library) Tutorials

    • Essential for 3D perception
    • Critical for robot mapping and object recognition

Motion Planning​

  • OMPL (Open Motion Planning Library)

    • Comprehensive motion planning algorithms
    • Important for robot navigation and manipulation

  • MoveIt Tutorials

    • Motion planning framework for manipulation
    • Essential for robotic arm control

Simulation​

  • Gazebo Tutorials

    • 3D simulation environment
    • Critical for robot development and testing

  • PyBullet Documentation

    • Physics simulation library
    • Useful for fast prototyping and reinforcement learning

Standards and Guidelines​

ROS Standards​

  • ROS Enhancement Proposals (REPs)

    • Official ROS standards and guidelines
    • Important for proper ROS development

  • ROS 2 Design Documents

    • Architectural decisions and design rationale
    • Essential for understanding ROS 2

Robotics Standards​

  • Robotics Standardization Roadmap (IEEE)

    • Industry standards for robotics
    • Important for commercial robot development

  • ISO Standards for Robotics

    • International standards for robot safety and performance
    • Critical for real-world robot deployment

Research Datasets​

Robotics Datasets​

  • Robotics Data Sets Repository (Radish)

    • Collection of robotics datasets
    • Useful for algorithm testing and validation

  • KITTI Vision Benchmark Suite

    • Benchmark for mobile robotics and autonomous driving
    • Important for perception algorithm evaluation

  • RobotWebTools Datasets

    • Collection of robotics-related datasets
    • Useful for various robotics applications

This curated list of resources provides a pathway for continued learning and research in Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics. We recommend starting with the foundational texts and gradually exploring more specialized resources based on your interests and applications.
